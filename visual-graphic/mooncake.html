Lunar Artistry

This is a mooncake packaging design I created for the Pacific Hotel in Yuyao City in June 2024 with graphic designer Gang Chen.

We chose to feature the works of Zhifo Chen, a Chinese painter from Yuyao, as the main element, adding some textual accents. Eating mooncakes during the Mid-Autumn Festival each year is a tradition of Chinese culture, and we hope that the creators of these traditional arts can also be recognized by more generations.
