A Journey to Selfhood

This brochure design explores the struggle between individuality and societal norms. Red represents the desire to stand out, while green reflects external pressures.

Across five collages, a journey from struggle to self-realization unfolds. Ropes, chains, and watchful eyes symbolize societal constraints, while edited figures of “normal” onlookers reflect judgment. Ultimately, the individual breaks free, embracing their true self and challenging the notion of normalcy.
