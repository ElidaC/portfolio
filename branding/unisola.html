Unisola

Unisola is a rebranding project that reinterprets Maija Isola's iconic Unikko design through a contemporary and sustainable lens. By using bamboo as the main material for the tote bag collection, the project highlights environmental responsibility while preserving the natural elegance of the original pattern of "Unikko".

The rebranding system includes a brand manual, logo design, product catalog, merchandise, website, motion graphics, and a promotional film. Together, these elements form a cohesive visual identity that bridges heritage and modern design, presenting Unikko in a fresh, relevant, and responsible way.
