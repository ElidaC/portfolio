Rainforest Essence

This board game teaches teens about sustainability and the impact of deforestation. Players represent skincare brands under Natura Co., working with Amazonian tribes to protect the rainforest. By using natural ingredients in skincare, the game shows how sustainable practices benefit both the environment and consumers. The goal is to raise funds to reclaim land, conserve biodiversity, and balance economic growth with nature and future generations' well-being.
