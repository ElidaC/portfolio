Vermilion Logo

Building on Vermilion's existing logo and visual identity, this project introduces a cut-out “V” motif that seamlessly integrates with the original mark. Designed specifically for social media, the logo highlights the power of digital platforms and their reciprocal relationship with Vermilion's brand growth.

Throughout the rebranding process, red remains the core color palette. Darker red tones are emphasized to create a sense of depth and advantage, unifying the visual language across podcast covers, testimonial posts, and inspirational quote posters while maintaining a strong and cohesive brand presence.
