Puffy Puff

Puffy Puff is a display typeface inspired by the world of baking. Its letterforms echo the soft, airy expansion of muffins, choux pastries and other delights fresh from the oven. Each character grows with a gentle roundness, capturing the moment a pastry rises and becomes irresistibly puffed. The flat bottoms of the letters create a grounded, tray-like alignment that brings a playful sense of order to the overall rhythm.
