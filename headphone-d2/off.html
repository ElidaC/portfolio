when feeling down at midnight

耳机对我来说像一扇门，轻轻一合，世界就被隔绝在外。

可我又害怕那门后面的寂静，

害怕音乐一响起，就只剩我和自己在那片无边的暗夜里。

旋律像是潮水，一遍遍拍打我心里最柔软的角落，

我不敢逃，却又贪恋那种被冲刷的感觉。

每一次戴上耳机，都像是坠入一个只有我能听见呼吸的宇宙。

那里没有人叫我的名字，也没有人需要我微笑，

但是有一股力量让我在这个世界里越陷越深。

我在那儿失重、碎裂、再一点点重组自己的另一副模样。


一个人的世界孤独吗？

也许吧。但有了这堵“墙”，我终于可以放下所有伪装，

在属于自己的小小宇宙里，自由地空白一会儿。

To me, headphones are like a door

that closes and shuts everything else out.

But I'm scared of the silence behind that door.

When the music starts, it's just me and myself in a dark, endless space.

The melody keeps hitting the softest parts of my heart, over and over.

I don't want to run, but somehow I like that feeling of being washed away.


Every time I put my headphones on,

it feels like falling into a tiny universe

where only I can hear my own breathing.

No one calls my name. No one expects me to smile.

Still, something keeps pulling me deeper into that world.

There, I fall apart a little,

and then slowly put myself back together again.


Is it lonely, being in your own world?

Maybe.

But with this invisible wall around me,

I can finally stop pretending and

just drift for a while in my own quiet space.
