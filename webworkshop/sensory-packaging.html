Sensory Packaging Generator

In this project, I transformed visual, tactile, and olfactory sensations into design elements and developed a packaging generator to redesign packaging for The Body Shop's 14 Body Butter products.

For future products with similar formulations, users can simply input the name, natural ingredients, two scents, skin type, and cream texture of the new product into a Google Sheet to generate the packaging for this series.
